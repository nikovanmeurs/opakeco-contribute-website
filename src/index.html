opakeco for charities

Bringing transparency and accountability to humanitarian causes

The opakeco platform

Showing donors what happens with the money they donate is difficult. Even though many charities regularly publish proceedings, many people hesitate to donate to causes due to a lack of transparency.

The Opakeco Foundation intends to change this through the development of an easy to use platform which can be used by people to donate to your charity and for you to spend funds received through donations. This makes it possible to achieve a high level of accountability without requiring technical skills and effort on your side.

Read the whitepaper

Why use the Opakeco Platform?

  • Extended target audience

    The transparency gained through use of the Opakeco Platform gives potential donors confidence that the funds they donate are well spent. This will enable people to donate who would otherwise hesitate to do so.

  • Effortless

    Using the Opakeco Platform will be as easy as ordering your food online. The Opakeco Foundation takes care of all the technological and legal hurdles so you don’t have to.

  • Fast

    Transferring funds across the world takes seconds instead of hours when using the Opakeco Platform, thereby helping you save precious time to get aid to where it’s needed most.

  • No cost

    The Opakeco Foundation intends to be able to sustain the cost of the platform through sponsoring and licensing (of technology?). As such, there are no costs for both donors and charities when making use of the platform.

  • Opakeco donates

    Most of the Opakeco Foundation’s yearly budget surplus will be donated to associated charities. The Opakeco Foundation reserves just enough funds be able to guarantee the sustainability of the platform.
